Our Story

A Pressing Challenge

From a young age, I was blessed with the opportunity to receive an exemplary education thanks to the unwavering support of my family. My childhood was filled with extensive travel that enriched my worldview, yet it was only upon settling in Gandigal with my spouse that I fully appreciated the privileges I had been afforded.

Upon recognizing the stark disparities in educational opportunities here, my spouse, Nathan, and I were compelled to act. Nathan, who has been an educator for nearly a decade, utilizes his expertise in French to bridge educational gaps in our community. Together, we envisioned a future where the children of Gandigal could access the kind of education that opens doors to boundless possibilities.

In pursuit of this vision, we have partnered with a Christian mission, which graciously provides us with a building at no cost. This facility has become the cornerstone of our educational project, allowing us to create a nurturing learning environment that reflects our commitment to the community’s growth

Our journey is a testament to the belief that with the right resources and community support, significant change is achievable. We are dedicated to being catalysts for this change, ensuring that every child in Gandigal can dream big and achieve even bigger.

-Grace Mbeko, Director
